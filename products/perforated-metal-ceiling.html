Product Description

Perforated Metal Ceiling Panels are architectural metal ceiling tiles with micro-precision perforations that provide acoustic absorption while maintaining clean modern aesthetic lines. The micro-perforations are invisible from normal viewing distances, creating the appearance of solid metal while delivering effective sound control. These panels are specified in airports, hospitals, and premium office developments worldwide.
