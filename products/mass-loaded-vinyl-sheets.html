Product Description

Mass Loaded Vinyl Sheets are dense, flexible sound blocking membranes that add mass to walls, floors, and ceilings to prevent sound from transmitting through building structures. Unlike rigid drywall, MLV sheets can be draped or wrapped around irregular shapes, making them ideal for pipes, ductwork, and structural elements where conventional rigid barriers cannot be easily installed.
