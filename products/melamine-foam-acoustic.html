Product Description

Melamine Foam Acoustic Panels are lightweight, cost-effective acoustic treatment panels made from open-cell melamine resin foam. Despite their affordable price point, these panels offer excellent broadband sound absorption performance and are widely used in recording studios, practice rooms, and commercial spaces where budget-conscious acoustic treatment is required.
