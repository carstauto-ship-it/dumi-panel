Resilient Channel Soundproofing Systems

Resilient Channel Soundproofing Systems use resilient metal channels to mechanically decouple drywall from the structural framing. This decoupling interrupts sound vibration transfer through the wall structure, providing significantly improved sound isolation without adding excessive mass. Resilient channels are a foundational technique in professional soundproofing and work best when combined with acoustic insulation and multiple layers of drywall.
