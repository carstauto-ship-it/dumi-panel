Mass Loaded Vinyl Soundproof Panels

Mass Loaded Vinyl (MLV) Soundproof Panels provide high-mass sound blocking in an exceptionally thin profile. These panels use dense vinyl compound loaded with barium salts or similar materials to create effective sound barriers. Ideal for applications where wall thickness is limited but significant sound blocking is required. MLV panels are flexible yet dense, conforming to surfaces while blocking airborne sound transmission.
