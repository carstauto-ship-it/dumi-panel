Marine Acoustic Panels

Marine Acoustic Panels are purpose-designed acoustic treatment products engineered for the unique challenges of marine environments — high humidity, salt spray, temperature extremes, and strict fire safety regulations. These panels utilize moisture-resistant cores, marine-grade finishes, and non-combustible or fire-retardant materials to deliver reliable acoustic performance on yachts, cruise ships, naval vessels, and offshore platforms.
