Product Description

Custom Shaped Acoustic Panels are precision CNC-cut acoustic panels manufactured to exact specifications for unique architectural installations where standard rectangular panels cannot fit or achieve the desired aesthetic effect. From circular ceiling clouds to triangular wall features to branded logo panels, these custom solutions enable architects and designers to incorporate acoustic treatment as an integral part of interior design.
